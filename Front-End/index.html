About Us

Welcome to Our Indian Kitchen, an online mess system especially for busy students and hardworking professionals seeking the comforting taste of home amidst their hectic schedules. At Our Indian Kitchen, we understand the daily struggles of juggling academics, work, and household responsibilities. That's why we embarked on a mission to provide wholesome home-cooked meals, conveniently packaged in traditional dabbas, to those who find themselves unable to prepare food at home. With Our Indian Kitchen, you can bid farewell to mealtime worries and savor the simple joy of a satisfying, wholesome meal delivered right to your doorstep.
